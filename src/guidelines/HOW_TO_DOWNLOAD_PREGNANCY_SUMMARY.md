2. Click on the **"Profile"** icon in the bottom navigation bar (far right)
3. The profile page will open

### Step 2: Go to Health Data Section
1. At the top of the profile page, you'll see four tabs:

2. Click on **"Health Data"** tab

### Step 3: Scroll to Find the Download Button
1. Scroll down through the health data section
2. You'll see:
   - Pregnancy Progress card (Week, Due Date, Days Left)
   - Health Metrics card (Weight, Height, BMI)
   - Emergency Contact card
3. Keep scrolling...

### Step 4: Download the Summary
1. At the bottom, you'll find a special card with:
   - A purple/pink gradient background
   - A document icon
   - Title: **"Pregnancy Summary Report"**
   - Description: "Download a comprehensive summary of your pregnancy journey..."
2. Click the blue button that says: **"Download Summary PDF"**

### Step 5: View Your PDF
1. The file will download as an HTML file (e.g., `Pregnancy-Summary-Brenda-Wanjiku-2025-11-04.html`)
2. Open the file in your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)

### Step 6: Print or Save as PDF (Optional)
1. In your browser, press `Ctrl+P` (Windows) or `Cmd+P` (Mac)
2. Select "Save as PDF" as the destination/printer
3. Click "Save" or "Print"
4. Now you have a proper PDF file!

## ğŸ”§ Troubleshooting

### PDF Not Downloading?
1. Check browser pop-up blocker settings
2. Try a different browser
3. Refresh the page and try again
4. Clear browser cache

### PDF Looks Wrong?
1. Make sure to open in a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
2. Don't open in text editors
3. Use "Print to PDF" for best results
4. Check your screen zoom level (should be 100%)

### Can't Find the Button?
1. Make sure you're on the Profile page (bottom navigation)
2. Click the "Health Data" tab (second tab from left)
3. Scroll all the way to the bottom
4. Look for the purple/pink gradient card

### Want to Customize?
The PDF currently uses mock data. In the full production version:
- It will use your actual pregnancy data
- Include real appointments from your calendar
- Show genuine milestones you've achieved
- Connect to your health records
